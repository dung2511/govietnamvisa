A General Vietnam visa fees information

For getting Vietnam E-Visa, you are required to pay only one kind of fee: E-Visa fee. With the fee of E-visa, you don't need to pay any more extra fee when you arrive at Vietnam entry port.

It is the fee that the applicants have to pay online for us to get the E-Visa from Vietnam Immigration Department. If you don’t have E-Visa, you can not enter Vietnam.

The fee is flexible because it depends on the type of visa you want to obtain and how fast you want and your country you are.
