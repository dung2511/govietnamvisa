1 How to stay in Vietnam longer?

 
There 2 ways to help you stay in Vietnam longer, you may use Vietnam visa extension or Vietnam visa run.


1.1. What is visa extension ?

Visa extension is an action that is applied for foreigners who wish to prolong their current stay in Vietnam without leaving the country. By this action, you will get an extension stamp on your passport indicating your new stay duration.
In the past, you can extend your stay in Vietnam easily but Vietnam currently only grants a single entry with 30 days validity. At the moment, Vietnam visa extension is not available, you can consider using our visa-run service.
 
 
1.2 . What is visa run?
 
Vietnam visa run is the same as Vietnam visa extension to help you are in Vietnam longer, which is considered the cheapest and fastest way to stay longer in Vietnam. For visa run, travellers must leave to Vietnam and come back with a new visa for an extended stay in the country.
 
If you use our visa run service, our staff will help you leave Vietnam, enter another neighbouring country (Cambodia, Laos or any other country you wish), do the quick immigration there and come back to Vietnam with the new 30 days visa within 4 hours – 1 day.
